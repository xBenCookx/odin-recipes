Mexican Street Tacos

Another quick and easy entree to make are tacos. They are very flavorful and made for almost everyone.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas - Some taco sized tortillas are the best for this meal, but you can use flour instead.
  • Skirt Steak - You will want to cut it into 1/4 inch chunks.
  • Orange Juice - This is a common overlooked ingredient, but freshly squeezed juice is a key component for the steak. Only about 1/4 would work.
  • Garlic, Chili Powder, Cumin, Oregano, and Salt - Seasonings are a huge part of the flavor for street tacos
  • Avocado Oil - This is to sear on your steak
  • Toppings - Onion, Cilantro, Salsa, and fresh Lime juice.

Additional Toppings

  • Cheese - Cheese is my favorite addition on anything. Especially tacos!
  • Try more toppings - Radishes, Cucumbers, Avocado, Pice de Gallo, Sour Cream, Lettuce, or even Cabbage
  • Make it spicy - Add some red peppers or jalapeños for a little kick.
  • Seasoning - Some taco seasoning is always great to add

Now it is time for the fun part!

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet and place one or two tortillas at a time into the pan.
  2. Warm until the tortillas is pliable and starting to brown. Wrap them in a kitchen towel to keep them warm while you cook your skirt steak.
  3. By now, your steak will be marinated by mixing the steak pieces with the orange juice, garllic chili powder, cumin, salt, and oregano.
  4. Place the steak in the fridge to marinate
  5. Add avocado oil to the skillet, over medium heat. When hot, add the marinated steak.
  6. Cook, stirring often, until the steak is deeply browned.
  7. Fill your tacos with steak and top with cilantro and onions. Serve with salsa and lime wedges to squeeze over the top.

Time to eat!
