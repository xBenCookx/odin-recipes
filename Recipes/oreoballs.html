Oreo Balls

Description

These are my favorite treats! I am a huge chocolate lover and thats what this treat is: chocolate galore!

Here are the ingredients, equipment, and recipe for this chocolate treat:

Ingredients

  • 38 Oreo cookies
  • 8 oz cream cheese - make sure this is at room temperature
  • 1 cup chocolate wafers
  • 1 cup of white chocolate wafers

Equipment

  • Food Processor
  • Stand Mixer
  • Baking Sheets
  • Parchment Paper

Instructions

  1. Put the oreo cookies in the food processor. Grind until they are fine crumbs. If you do not have a food processor, you could also place the cookies in a ziploc bag, and use a roller to crush them.
  2. Beat the cream cheese until it is smooth in the stand mixer, or by hand. Add the cookie crumbs and mix it until well combined.
  3. Roll this mixture into small balls and put them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Freeze the oreo balls for 15 minutes.
  4. Place the chocolate wafers in a microwave safe bowl and melt the wafers per package instructions.
  5. Remove the balls from the freezer and dip them into the melted chocolate. Make sure to cover them completely. If you want to, sprinkle excess crumbs or the opposite color chocolate on top of them. Lay the balls on the parchment paper.
  6. Place the tray with the balls in the fridge for at least an hour. Keep in an airtight container and they will last weeks.

Once complete, you will have one of the best chocolate treats known to man (or just know to me). Enjoy!
